About Me

I am a Senior Mechanical Design Engineer with over 10 years of experience delivering complex electromechanical products across defence, avionics, RF systems, consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial electronics integration. I hold an MSc in Mechatronics Embedded Systems from the University of Southern Denmark and a CSWP certification in SolidWorks.

Throughout my career I have led and contributed to over 20 R&D programmes, taking products from initial concept through to production across demanding industries where precision, reliability, and environmental robustness are non-negotiable. My work spans airborne defence systems, high-power RF amplifiers, precision measurement equipment, and automotive sensor integration, each requiring a rigorous engineering approach and close collaboration with electronics, manufacturing, and cross-functional teams.

I bring both technical depth and broad industrial experience to every project, with a consistent record of delivering designs that meet stringent performance requirements and enter production successfully.

Projects & Work

IFF Transponder and Interrogator

Designed a modular CNC-milled aluminium chassis for an airborne IFF system, integrating transmitter, receiver, power supply, and digital cards within tight space and weight constraints. Slot-fitting PCB cards with wedge locks and integrated airflow channels ensured thermal performance and serviceability. Qualified through environmental testing and entered production at 100 units.

High Power Amplifier Chassis

Designed CNC-milled aluminium chassis with integrated heat sinks for a high-power RF amplifier system. Cooling fin geometry was optimised through coupled CFD and FEA thermal simulation to meet dissipation targets under worst-case load. RF isolation between stages was maintained through coordinated internal module layout. Thermal targets were met and the design entered production at 50 units.

Standby Flight Information Display Unit

Designed a precision chassis integrating an LCD, gyroscope, and PCB within extremely tight size and weight constraints. EMC and EMI gaskets with controlled apertures were applied for electromagnetic compatibility. An air-sealed mechanical pathway was engineered at the rear intake to prevent pressure leakage and maintain accurate sensor readings. Design completed R&D and entered production at 10 units.

Precision Magnetic Flux Test Bench

Designed and developed a precision magnetic flux test bench for permanent magnet motor rotors, commissioned by SECOP as part of an MSc thesis at SDU. The system combined mechanical design using non-magnetic materials, motor control for repeatable rotor rotation, Hall effect sensor positioning at 0.01mm resolution, and automated data acquisition and analysis in Python. Results were used for rotor magnetisation evaluation and weak magnet area identification on SPMSM and IPMSM motors.

Excavator Sensor Mounting System

Designed aluminium sheet metal mounting structures for sensor, camera, and antenna systems on excavators, engineered for outdoor durability with UV and chemical resistant surface treatments. Fool-proof assembly features including asymmetric geometry and keying ensured correct field installation without engineering support. Design entered production with 55 units delivered and a 45-unit follow-on order in progress.

Consumer Electronics Products

Designed injection-moulded housings for POS terminals, handheld Android devices, fingerprint scanner enclosures, and battery bank casings within tight dimensional envelopes and cost constraints driven by high-volume production. Seal geometry and material selection were applied to achieve IP ratings on applicable products. Designs were validated through iterative FDM prototyping in ABS, PLA, and PETG before tooling commitment, with production volumes ranging from 500 to 1500 units per product.
